Support / Resistance Guide
VP levels: price zones where most shares traded -- support from above, resistance from below 52W Low / High: major annual support and resistance 50/200-day MA: dynamic support/resistance Round numbers: psychological S/R High put OI: gravitational support via market maker hedging High call OI: resistance ceiling via market maker hedging VIX below 15: thin premiums VIX 15-20: normal conditions VIX 20-30: elevated, favorable for put selling VIX above 30: fear spike, exceptional premium, higher risk VIX backwardation (VIX above VIX3M): historically strong time to sell puts
Income Strategy
TARGET YIELD: 12% annualized per transaction HORIZON: 1.5 to 3 months out OTM BUFFER: 5-12% preferred LIQUIDITY: monthly OI above 1000 contracts PUTS: sell when RSI oversold, lower 52W range, near VP support, no near-term earnings CALLS: sell when RSI overbought, upper 52W range, near VP resistance, own the shares APY puts: premium / (strike x 100) x (365/DTE) x 100 APY calls: premium / (price x 100) x (365/DTE) x 100 IVR above 60: elevated IV, good time to sell options IVR below 30: compressed IV, wait for better conditions Implied Move: ATM straddle / price -- markets expected earnings move SIZING: look-through strike value aligned with portfolio CASH: park premiums in SGOV pending redeployment
