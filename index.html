Support / Resistance
S1: nearest volume support below price (strongest short-term floor) S2-S5: additional supports, progressively weaker R1: nearest resistance above price (immediate ceiling) R2-R5: additional resistances above R1 Volume Profile (VP): price zones where most shares traded -- act as both support (from above) and resistance (from below) 52W Low: major annual support, break below is a warning 52W High: major resistance, break above triggers momentum 50/200-day MA: dynamic support/resistance, widely watched Round numbers: psychological S/R ($50, $100, $150 etc) High put OI: gravitational support via market maker hedging High call OI: resistance ceiling via market maker hedging VIX < 15: thin premiums VIX 15-20: normal conditions VIX 20-30: elevated, favorable for put selling VIX > 30: fear spike, exceptional premium, higher risk VIX backwardation (VIX > VIX3M): acute near-term fear, historically strong time to sell puts
Income Strategy
TARGET YIELD: 12% annualized per transaction HORIZON: 1.5 to 3 months out (nimble, active) OTM BUFFER: 5-12% preferred (balanced risk/reward) LIQUIDITY: monthly OI > 1000 contracts PUTS: sell when RSI oversold, lower 52W range, price near VP support, no near-term earnings CALLS: sell when RSI overbought, upper 52W range, price near VP resistance, own the shares APY CALC (puts): premium / (strike x 100) x 365/DTE x 100 APY CALC (calls): premium / (current price x 100) x 365/DTE x 100 SIZING: look-through strike value aligned with portfolio CASH: park premiums in SGOV pending redeployment RISK: avoid earnings windows, watch beta for OTM sizing AWARENESS: Thu expiry possible if Fri is a holiday
