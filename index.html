Income Engine Framework
LAYER 1 -- RISK-FREE BASELINE: 3-month and 6-month T-bills Current yield sets your opportunity cost floor. All other layers should earn a meaningful risk premium above this. LAYER 2 -- OPTIONS INCOME ETFs: SPYI + NBOS SPYI: covered calls on S&P 500 components -- equity upside participation + call premium income NBOS: put-writing strategy with defined buffers -- premium income with downside protection Target: track trailing 12-month yield vs T-bill spread LAYER 3 -- ACTIVE WHEEL STRATEGY: Put selling + Covered calls Target: 12% annualized APY per transaction Tools: conviction dashboards, VP support/resistance, OI analysis, RSI, earnings calendar Risk management: 5-12% OTM buffer, no earnings straddle, beta-aware sizing
Support / Resistance Guide
VP levels: price zones where most shares traded -- support from above, resistance from below 52W Low / High: major annual support and resistance 50/200-day MA: dynamic support/resistance Round numbers: psychological S/R High put OI: gravitational support via market maker hedging High call OI: resistance ceiling via market maker hedging VIX below 15: thin premiums VIX 15-20: normal -- standard conditions VIX 20-30: elevated -- favorable for put selling VIX above 30: fear spike -- exceptional premium, higher risk VIX backwardation (VIX above VIX3M): historically strong time to sell puts
